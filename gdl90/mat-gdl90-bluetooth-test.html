🛩️ MAT GDL90 Bluetooth Connection

Connect to ADS-B receivers via Bluetooth for weather and traffic data

📡 Connection

Before Connecting:

  1. Pair your ADS-B device with your computer/phone via system Bluetooth settings
  2. For GDL 50/52: Enable Bluetooth on the device and pair it
  3. Click "Connect Bluetooth" and select your device from the list
  4. Some devices may require selecting a specific COM port

🔌 Bluetooth Serial (SPP)

Garmin GDL 50/52, Dual XGPS, Bad Elf, most ADS-B receivers

📶 Bluetooth Low Energy

Some newer BLE-based receivers (experimental)
